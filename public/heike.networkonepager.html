Introduction to heike.network

The global consulting market is dominated by large players that have the international organizational structure, brand, a working internal bureaucracy, the width and breadth of skilled workers organized as effective teams and a well oiled sales force that can profit from the knowledge economy. They depend on attracting talent from a fluid market of highly skilled people widely distributed geographically.

The internal bureaucracy equivalent of these large organizations can be fully automated, lowering significantly overhead resulting in an accelerated fully digital deal flow for even the smallest community. Self-organizing communities offer entrepreneurs and freelancers a sense of belonging and connection that helps establish trust and generate opportunities in lieu of costly marketing and business development.

Projects require capital, talents and vision to become businesses. heike.network organizes the first two in the real and digital world to offer a fertile ground to support the third.

Goal

Turn the bootstrapping process of finding funding and setting up engineering teams from months long process to days where the project owners and contributors reduce financial risks through a sustainable transparent digital economy where money is turned into profitable businesses and profit is returned back to the communities of people to allow them to prosper collectively.

Mechanism

heike.network is a software system for independent legal entities worldwide to federate and manage a network of people and projects. The system provides full transparency and accountability by automating escrowing of tokenized fiat funds and accounting of transactions on a consensus-backed ledger. Work is done on a simplified collaboration model favoring colocation and the rule of an hour worked is an hour paid.

Features

  • [Layer 1] Community: learn, share, grow
  • Access and organize collective resources, from co-living to co-working space to events
  • Benefit from the collective reinvestment into growth of a worldwide network of franchises
  • Turn your learning into a project and receive the individualized, 1:1 help of mentors
  • [Layer 2] Work: contribute, build, make a living
  • Find interesting funded projects and offer your skills to make a living
  • Provide a tamper proof history of compensated work as your portfolio to new clients
  • Find and quickly join funded projects that are of interest to you in the location of your choice
  • [Layer 3] Entrepreneurship: start, grow, profit
  • Create new ventures, find funding and build teams with opportunities worldwide
  • Easily search for and invite talent to work on projects with long-term funding
  • Manage your project costs in real-time, contractual relationships digitally

  • Project owners: sign up, create a digital identity and setup project escrows. Deposit funds at a franchise of your choice in any fiat currencies to internally traded tokens with real-time preferred currency conversion. Fund project, approve resources and project spending in real-time.
  • Freelancers: sign up, create a digital identity, create a profile and target hourly rate. Apply for projects or get invited to projects and record work once approved. Receive fiat currency compensation on demand or use the digital tokens equivalent to purchase internal services. Get support from franchises and the network of franchises to mediate conflicts.
  • Franchisees: create your own franchised community with our open source software and customize  to your local legal requirements. Recruit freelancers and project owners using a known brand with a transparent process to track work and compensation. Bootstrap teams by hosting digital nomads.
